TradeUp invests in your future with ISAs! We’re with you every step of the way.

Instead of traditional loans, we offer a new type of financing called an Income Share Agreement (or ISA). Unlike a loan, you only pay back income share agreements once you land a job!

TradeUp is invested in your education, your job search, and your future. From interview help and resume reviews to dedicated student success managers TradeUp works with you to make sure you’re prepared for your career. We only succeed when you succeed.

Income Share Agreement Benefits

No Job, No Payment

If you don’t land a job making above your salary floor, your payments drop to zero. Payments also adjust with your income to stay affordable when you are employed.

Never Pay Too Much

When you do land your dream job, we set a limit on how much you’ll pay back. Our ISAs have a hard cap, and after you hit it your ISA is over!

Fixed Payment Period

Unlike a loan, ISAs don't go on forever. Once your ISA period is over, your payments stop, even if you’ve paid back less than you borrowed.

No Co-signers Required

You don’t need a credit score, co-signer, or collateral to apply. TradeUp evaluates applications on your future potential, not your past.

Deferred Payments

ISAs have built in protection if you need to stop working. If you have an emergency or get laid off, payments are paused automatically. No fees and no penalties.

No Interest, Ever

ISAs have no principal and no interest. This means there is no minimum repayment amount: you may even pay back less than you borrowed.

Heres how it works!

Apply online in less than 10 minutes

Our application is fast and simple. You can apply once you’ve been accepted to one of our schools, or you can browse our programs to find one that’s right for you. Not sure what career is best for you? Use our live chat feature to chat with a career counselor.

Get accepted and get your terms!

Our typical acceptance time is 2-4 business days, but you could be accepted within an hour! Once you get accepted to your program of choice and to TradeUp’s ISA program, you’re ready to start your training. Your tuition will be sent directly to your school.

Get a great education,
pay only when you land a job!

TradeUp is invested in you, since we only make money once you get a job. That means that we vet our schools to make sure they provide you with a quality education. All of our partner schools provide top notch training that will help you to secure a job after graduating.

Land a high paying
job and start repayment

Your payments only start once you land a job and are making above $25,000. That’s the “income share” in Income Share Agreements! If you lose your job, or your salary drops below the threshold, your payments are deferred automatically, no fees, no penalties.
